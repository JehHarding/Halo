Behind Halo

Born from love.
Built from necessity.

Halo began with Angel — a pet who defied every prognosis — and the realisation that no app existed that could hold everything her care required.

Three heart conditions. Three daily medications. Blood tests every three months. An annual cardiac scan. And a vet's prognosis that gave her two weeks, two months, or two years.

That was six years ago. Angel is still here.
