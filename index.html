Behind Halo

Angel is the reason.

Angel is the reason Halo exists. She was given two weeks, two months, or two years. That was seven years ago. Managing her care taught me that pets with complex needs deserve more than a notes app and a hope you remember. I built Halo for her, and for every pet owner carrying the weight of a complicated routine, because I know exactly how heavy that gets.

Questions

Things people ask.

When is Halo launching? +
We're in the final stages of development. Join the waitlist and you'll be the first to know, no newsletters, no noise. Just one message when Halo is ready.
Is Halo iOS only, or is Android coming? +
iOS first. Android is on the roadmap and waitlist members will be the first to hear when a date is confirmed.
How much will Halo cost? +
Pricing will be announced at launch. Waitlist members will get early access and will hear about it first.
Can I use Halo for more than one pet? +
Yes. Each pet gets their own complete profile, medications, vitals, contacts, calendar, and carer's pack, all in one place.
Can I share my pet's profile with a vet or carer? +
Yes. The carer's pack lets you share your pet's complete care guide, medications, routine, emergency contacts, and everything a carer needs, in one tap.
Is my pet's health data private? +
Completely. Your data belongs to you. It is never shared, sold, or used for advertising, ever.
My pet is healthy. Do I still need Halo? +
Halo works just as well for healthy pets, vaccination records, vet contacts, routine reminders, and a carer's pack ready for whenever you travel. The peace of mind isn't only for complex cases.
