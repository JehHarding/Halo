Privacy Policy

Last updated: 23 June 2026

Halo ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what personal data we collect, why we collect it, and how we use and protect it. It applies to the Halo website (haloapp.co.uk) and the Halo iOS application.

Halo is operated by Marie Jehane Juganaikloo (trading as Jehane Harding), trading as Halo, based in the United Kingdom. We are the data controller for the purposes of UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018.

1. What data we collect

On our website:

  • Email address, if you join our waitlist
  • Usage data collected via cookies (pages visited, time on site, browser type). See our Cookie Policy for details.

In the Halo app:

  • Information you enter about your pet: name, medications, dosages, appointment details, weight and breathing rate readings, and notes
  • Photos you choose to upload of your pet
  • Contact information you add to your Carer's Pack (such as vet details and emergency contacts)

We do not collect any data about you personally within the app beyond what you voluntarily enter. We do not collect human health data.

2. How we use your data

  • To provide and operate the Halo app and website
  • To contact you about your waitlist position and the Halo launch
  • To improve and develop Halo based on how it is used
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Legal basis for processing

Under UK GDPR, we process your data on the following legal bases:

  • Consent — when you join the waitlist or accept cookies
  • Legitimate interests — to operate, maintain and improve our services, where your interests and rights do not override ours
  • Legal obligation — where we are required to process data to comply with the law

4. Data storage and security

Pet health data entered in the Halo app is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit or store this data on external servers at this time. Where data is stored remotely in future, we will update this policy and ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.

Waitlist email addresses are held securely and are not shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

5. How long we keep your data

  • Waitlist email addresses are held until you unsubscribe or request deletion, or until Halo launches and the waitlist is no longer active
  • Data stored on your device within the app is retained until you delete the app or clear its data

6. Sharing your data

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with trusted third-party service providers who help us operate our services (such as email delivery services), under strict data processing agreements. We may also disclose data if required to do so by law.

7. Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Object to or restrict how we process your data
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@haloapp.co.uk. We will respond within 30 days.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the UK Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

8. Cookies

Our website uses cookies. For full details of the cookies we use and how to manage them, please see our Cookie Policy.

9. Children

Halo is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify waitlist members of any material changes by email. The date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent version.

11. Contact us

For any questions about this policy or how we handle your data:
