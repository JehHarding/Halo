Privacy Policy

Last updated: 23 June 2026

Halo ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what personal data we collect, why we collect it, and how we use and protect it. It applies to the Halo website (haloapp.co.uk) and the Halo iOS application.

We are the data controller for the purposes of UK GDPR, the Data Protection Act 2018, and the Data (Use and Access) Act 2025.

1. What data we collect

On our website:

  • We do not currently use analytics or tracking cookies on this website.

In the Halo app:

  • Information you enter about your pet: name, medications, dosages, appointment details, weight and breathing rate readings, and notes
  • Photos you choose to upload of your pet
  • Contact information you add to your Carer's Pack, such as vet details and emergency contacts

All data entered in the app is stored locally on your device. We do not collect personal health data about you, and we do not transmit pet health data to our servers.

2. How we use your data

  • To operate the Halo app and website
  • To send occasional product updates under legitimate interests (UK GDPR Article 6(1)(f)). You may opt out at any time by contacting us directly.
  • To improve and develop Halo based on aggregated, anonymised usage patterns
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Legal basis for processing

Under UK GDPR Article 6, we process your data on the following bases:

  • Consent (Article 6(1)(a)) — when you accept optional cookies
  • Legitimate interests (Article 6(1)(f)) — to operate, maintain, and improve our services, where your interests and rights do not override ours
  • Legal obligation (Article 6(1)(c)) — where required by law, including HMRC record-keeping requirements

4. Data storage and security

Pet health data entered in the Halo app is stored locally on your device using Apple's on-device storage. It is not transmitted to Halo's servers. If you delete the app, all locally stored data is removed.

Waitlist email addresses are held securely and are not shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

We have a documented procedure for responding to data breaches. In the event of a reportable breach, we will notify the ICO within 72 hours and affected individuals without undue delay.

Data type Retention period Reason

6. Sharing your data

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share your data with third-party advertisers. We may share data with:

  • Apple Inc. — the Halo app runs on Apple's iOS platform. App data is stored using Apple's on-device frameworks. Apple's privacy policy applies to Apple's own services. Apple does not receive your pet health data from Halo.
  • Authorities — where required to do so by law.

7. International data transfers

Where personal data is transferred outside the UK, we ensure it is protected by appropriate safeguards, including the UK International Data Transfer Agreement (UK IDTA) or Standard Contractual Clauses. Apple Inc. is based in the United States; transfers to Apple are covered by the UK extension to the EU-US Data Privacy Framework.

8. Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you (Article 15)
  • Rectification — request correction of inaccurate data (Article 16)
  • Erasure — request deletion of your data, subject to legal retention requirements (Article 17)
  • Restriction — request that we limit how we process your data (Article 18)
  • Portability — request your data in a structured, machine-readable format (Article 20)
  • Object — object to processing based on legitimate interests, including direct marketing (Article 21)
  • Withdraw consent — at any time where processing is based on consent (Article 7(3))
  • No automated decisions — not to be subject to solely automated decision-making that significantly affects you (Article 22)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@haloapp.co.uk. We will respond within 30 days, free of charge.
